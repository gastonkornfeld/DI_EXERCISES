Fun Facts about Pets

  • Dogs may pretend to be sick to get more attention from their owners
  • Cats sleep for around 70% of their lives which can be up to 16 hours a day.
  • Dogs can smell when their owners are afraid thanks to their perspiration.
  • A cat’s brain is 90% similar to the human brain.
  • A study at UCSD claims that dogs can get jealous when their owners show fondness toward other creatures.
  • Happy rabbits like to hop and do a twist in the air...this act is also known as a “binky”.
  • Cats can rotate their ears up to 180º.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Humans and chimpanzees share 95 to 98 percent of the same DNA.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat
  • Chimpanzees and other species, including some types of birds, make and use tools.
  • Horses have around 205 bones in their skeleton.
  • Because horse's eyes are on the side of their head they are capable of seeing nearly 360 degrees at one time.
